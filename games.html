Our Games

Greyfell Studios is focused on building tense, cooperative experiences set in imaginative worlds. Below are our current projects and concepts.

Featured Project: Project Cobalt

Co-op Survival Extraction Shooter

Project Cobalt is a cooperative survival game set on storm-battered, post-nuclear islands. Players sail between zones, scavenge for resources, face mutated threats, and decide when to extract with their haul or push their luck for more.

  • 1–4 player online co-op
  • Procedurally generated islands and encounters
  • Persistent progression and gear risked on every run

Project Highlights

Microcosm

Microcosm is a small-scale world simulation where players care for a contained ecosystem inside a glass vessel. Every decision—adding species, adjusting climate, or introducing new technology—changes the balance of the world in unexpected ways.

Untitled Arena Shooter

This project explores a fast, minimal-HUD arena shooter inspired by classic competitive games. Short, high-stakes rounds and tight three-lane maps keep every match intense and replayable.
